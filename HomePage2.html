College Payment Support Site

The College Payment Support Site is designed to assist students of the College of Commerce Art and Science Patna by tackling prevalent payment issues encountered during college transactions. It features real-time updates, a dedicated student portal, and a notice board for sharing both problems and solutions, ensuring that students receive timely support and information.
