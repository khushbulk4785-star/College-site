College of Commerce, Arts & science, Patna, established in 1949, recognized under Sections 2 (f) and 12 (B) of the UGC Act 1956 is a premier constituent College of Patliputra University. College was established by great visionary Late Pt. Indu Shekhar Jha on the advice of Dr. Rajendra Prasad with a pious motive to make quality education accessible for aspiring youths without any discrimination based on socio-cultural-economic grounds. The vision of its great founder Pt. Indushekhar Jha was to make the commerce education accessible to the underprivileged and subaltern students of Bihar, which was rarely available.
